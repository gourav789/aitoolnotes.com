4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and gather usage data.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences and understand how you interact with our site.

Types of Cookies We Use:

  • Essential Cookies — Required for the website to function properly. These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics Cookies — Help us understand how visitors navigate our website (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Preference Cookies — Remember your settings and preferences for a better experience.
  • Advertising Cookies — Used to show you relevant ads based on your interests (e.g., Google AdSense, if applicable).
  • Third-Party Cookies — Set by external services embedded in our site, such as YouTube videos, social share buttons, or comment systems.

Your Cookie Choices

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View what cookies are stored
  • Delete cookies at any time
  • Block cookies from specific or all websites

For more information on managing cookies, visit your browser's help section or www.allaboutcookies.org.
